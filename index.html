One Year Later, Nepalis Reflect on Delayed Response to Massive Quake

Here in the ancient capital of Bhaktapur, ominous signs of last years' quake indicate the enormous amount of recovery work still needed. More than 8,000 people died in the 2015 disaster, leaving hundreds of thousands more in shelters awaiting government relief. But reconstruction has yet to arrive, leaving most of those affected still struggling with their daily lives.
