US Minorities Link Guns with Other Social Ills

African-Americans and Latinos believe fixing the gun violence crisis in the U.S. would also help resolve other societal ills that are plaguing America, new research shows. Research released by Benenson Strategy Group (BSG) found that a reduction in gun violence could help lower America's incarceration rate - the world's highest - and improve relationships between police and citizens in minority communities.
